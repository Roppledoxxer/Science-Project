Thermochemistry Notes

Exothermic Reactions

Exothermic reactions release heat energy and it can make the surroundings warmer.

  • Combustion
  • Neutralization
  • Freezing

The energy of products are lower than reactants.

Endothermic Reactions

Endothermic Reactions absorb energy from the surroundings and makes the surroundings cooler.

  • Photosynthesis
  • Thermal decomposition
  • Melting

The energy of products are higher than reactants.

Activation Energy

Activation Energy is the energy needed for a reaction to start.
